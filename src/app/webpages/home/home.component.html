About the NISWASEC

To excel in providing of portable, reliable and affordable pipe borne water and sewage disposal services to our customers in the Niger State on a sustainable basis while accountability and transparency of our services to the next level, the need for a technology driven solution cannot be over emphasized.

Niger Water and Sewage Corporation (NISWASEC) Billing and Settlement Solution is a software application built for the administration of water utilities and sewage control. It is user-friendly online portal designed with the capacity to verify existing and onboard new consumers, generate bills, notify consumers of their bills and process payments seamlessly.
