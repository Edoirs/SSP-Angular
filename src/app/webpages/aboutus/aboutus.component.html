About Us

Our Goal for Niger Water and Sewage Corporation

Vision

To be a world class agency in providing portable and sustainable water supply to every household and industry in Niger State.

Mission

Our mission is to provide clean water to urban and semi-urban areas, including small towns, in Niger State for domestic and industrial purposes, reducing waterborne diseases, poverty, and increasing productivity.
